Fair questions

FAQ

What if I don't like the website you built?

Then you pay nothing. Not a kill fee, not a "small deposit" — zero. We take it down and wish you well. Building it first is our risk, on purpose. It's how we'd want to be sold to.

How can you build it before ever talking to me?

Your business already has a public story — your reviews, your listings, what your customers rave about. We start there. After you say yes, we sit down with you and correct every price, photo, and detail before it goes on your own domain.

Why is this so much cheaper than an agency?

We're local, we're small, and we've built modern tools that do the heavy lifting — so an afternoon of our work looks like a month of theirs. The savings are real and they're yours.

What counts as an "update"?

Price changes, menu items, hours, photos, announcements — the everyday stuff, whenever you need it. Full redesigns and major new features (like online ordering) aren't part of monthly maintenance — those get a friendly quote first. Fixing anything broken is always free.

Do I actually own it?

The parts that matter are yours from day one: your domain is registered in your name, and your content — words, photos, prices — always belongs to you. The $99/month is simply the service: hosting, upkeep, and text-us-anything updates, for as long as you want them. After six months of service, the website itself is yours too — leave any time after that and we hand you the complete site files within 14 days, free, to host anywhere. Want the files before six months? We'll work out a fair one-time buyout. Either way, your domain and your content never stay behind.

How fast will it be live?

You've already seen it live. After you say yes, it's on your own domain, with your corrected prices, within about 48 hours.
